Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest gym membership in 2026?
Based on our data, 24 Hour Fitness offers some of the lowest reported base monthly rates starting around $18, though chains like Planet Fitness ($25) and Retro Fitness ($25) are also extremely competitive.
How much does a Planet Fitness membership cost?
Planet Fitness typically charges a base monthly rate of around $25 for their classic membership. Premium (Black Card) memberships usually cost more but provide additional benefits like guest privileges.
Are initiation fees common for gym memberships?
Yes, initiation fees are very common, though many gyms waive them during promotional periods (like January or holiday sales). When charged, they can range from $49 to $99 or more depending on the chain.
How much is an Equinox membership?
Equinox is a premium tier gym. While rates vary by location, our collected data shows starting base rates around $55, though full access or all-club access often pushes into the $200-$300+ range per month.
Do gym memberships require long-term contracts?
Many gyms now offer month-to-month options, but long-term contracts (typically 12 months) are still common. Choosing a 12-month contract often lowers the monthly rate or waives the initiation fee.

Methodology

Data was collected via automated scripts scraping information from various sources:

  • Official gym chain websites (when prices are publicly listed)
  • Aggregator sites like GymMembershipFees.com
  • Community reports from Reddit (r/fitness) for actual paid prices

Note on Missing Data: Gym prices vary wildly by location and current promotions. Where exact numbers could not be reliably verified, we have chosen to display "N/A" (null) rather than providing inaccurate estimates.
